Red Sauce Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pasta
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil or butter (divided) (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds(jeera)
  • 3 cloves garlic chopped finely
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (+ 1/2 tsp for boling pasta)
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala or pav bhaiji masala or any other masala powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon kasuri methi (or 2 tbsps coriander leaves)
  • 2 teaspoon cream as needed (optional)
  • 1 large onion chopped finely
  • 3/4 to 1 cup tomatoes chopped mashed or pureed (do not use very sour tomatoes)
  • 2 carrots chopped or as needed
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/4 cup capsium (bell pepper)-chopped
